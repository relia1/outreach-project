Outreach Project

There are many models which can describe sustainability, a common method is the three pillars of sustainability.[1] We can apply these models to how energy is generated and see how sustainable they are. Ideally sustainable energy generation can be viewed as a way to generate energy that does not take away from the environment, society, or the economy. Depending on what your views are, it is possible to have ideologies of sustainability that do not maintain all 3 pillars. This website will go on to explore different types of energy and additional information regarding energy and also pros and cons of the types of energy generation. e.g. Maybe it is cheaper to initially produce, or potentially it has a substantial environmental impact. I will later explore 3 additional sections as well. Such as pandemic impact, actions that could be taken, and my beliefs/opinion on moral obligations regarding energy sustainability.
